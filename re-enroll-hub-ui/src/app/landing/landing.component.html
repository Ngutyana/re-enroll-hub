ABOUT

This platform is an opportunity for students that deregistered during covid-19 and post-covid-19 due to the education transition, as students had gotten used to studying digitally during the pandemic and having to return to face-to-face studying post covid-19. Some students were affected by the transition as they could not as their situations could not accommodate them to relocate back to the cities where the institutions they were enrolled to for attending face-to-face classes and complete their studies. This platform is the centre of South African Universities and students impacted to from alliances to secure the future of students in need to complete their studies but can not relocate.


HOW IT WORKS

Impacted students that are interested in this program will register and apply, their applications will be reviewed by the university the student is enrolled to, to check whether the student meets the application requirements; if the students do meet the requirements, they will be enrolled to further their studies in the institution and be facilitated for online learning. The institution will ali with another one to facilitate the student with an examination hall closer to their residential area so they may be inivigilated to ensure authenticity during student examinations.
